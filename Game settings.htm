2  Game settings

 

Before you can begin playing your chosen game, you need to select your options from the four categories in the settings screen.

 

 

1.     Game Difficulty

The game difficulty determines the difficulty of the game, but not the difficulty of the words. That is handled by the sub-categories for large lists of words

·         Easy

o   Time limit: OFF

o   Incorrect Answers penalty: OFF

·         Medium

o   Time limit: ON

o   Incorrect Answers penalty: OFF

·         Hard

o   Time limit: ON

o   Incorrect Answers penalty: ON

 

2.     Word Category

Word Category selects the kinds of words you’ll be quizzed on.

·         Common Nouns (large)

·         Common Verbs (large)

·         Common Adjectives (large)

·         Numbers (large)

·         Travel (large)

·         Family (medium)

·         Phrases (medium)

·         Activities (medium)

·         Pronouns (small)

·         Colours (small)

·         Countries (small) 

 

3.     Sub-category

Sub-category displays different options depending on the size of the word category.

 

For large categories, the words are separated into difficulty, giving a more diverse selection of skill testing.

 

For the medium categories, the words are separated into set 1 and set 2 – there is no difference in difficulty between these sets.

 

Finally, for the small categories, there is only one set of words.

 

·         Large

o   Easy

o   Medium

o   Hard

·         Medium

o   Set 1

o   Set 2

·         Small

o   All Words

 

 

4.     Question Format

Question format determines the language of the input and output of the game.

 

·         Foreign to English

o   A word of your chosen language is displayed on screen, and you must answer with the word’s English translation.

·         English to Foreign

o   An English word is displayed on screen, and you must answer with the translation in your chosen language.
